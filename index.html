OUR SCHEDULE

HAPPY IEEE DAY!!

IEEE Day, the day that marked the new beginning to change the world through technology and community work was first acknowledged in 1884 .

  • "While the world benefits from what’s new, IEEE focuses on what’s next!. IEEE Day is moreover a celebration that successive generations of IEEE members and volunteers have been doing for more than a century
  • This year we celebrate the 12th annual IEEE Day with the theme “Leveraging Technology for a Better Tomorrow" on 04th Oct .
  • IEEE Day is lauded on First Tuesday in Oct , the gala of events remain active for many days and this year in between 25th September and 17th Oct.

We as IEEE representatives hallmark this day to pass the significance of IEEE and its objectives. We continue to build on the number of activities held around the world, and expand the local opportunities for IEEE members and colleagues to collaborate.

Motto of IEEE DAY 2022

01

IEEE Day is set for its commemoration on 04th of October 2022 in GVPCE under the supervision of IEEE Student Branch GVPCE(A). On this occasion we are set to explore on the theme --- “Leveraging Technology for a Better Tomorrow".
